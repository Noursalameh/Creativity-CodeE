About Creativity Code


At creativity Code , we bring imagination to life. Based in Jordan, our team specializes in transforming spaces through creative design and the innovative use of WPC — a material that combines the warmth of wood with the durability of modern composites.


We believe that great design is not only about aesthetics, but also about sustainability and functionality. With every project, we aim to create environments that inspire, comfort, and endure.

Guest House Petra

We have completed WPC pergola system covering guest rooms, restaurant, and outdoor lounges. Seamlessly blending modern durability with Petra’s timeless heritage, creating shaded, elegant spaces that enhance the guest experience while ensuring long-lasting performance in extreme desert conditions.

The Blessed Tree – Ministry of Awqaf

Sacred protective structure around Jordan’s historic Blessed Tree. Full WPC cladding on a custom metal frame, combining reverence with cutting-edge material technology to preserve this spiritual landmark for generations while maintaining natural wood aesthetics.

Garden Pergola Paradise

Create stunning outdoor living spaces with our custom pergola designs. Combining traditional craftsmanship with innovative WPC materials for structures that are both beautiful and built to last.

Why Choose Our Products

Why you need to know about WPC?

WPC is an innovative eco-friendly material combining wood fibers and recycled plastic for exceptional durability and natural aesthetics.

Superior Performance

Our WPC products are UV-resistant, water-resistant, and termite-proof, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Sustainable Choice

WPC products are sustainable and help reduce deforestation while providing the beauty of natural wood with enhanced performance.
